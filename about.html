What is a Nicole?

“A rock pile ceases to be a rock pile the moment a single man contemplates it, bearing within him the image of a cathedral.” ― Antoine de Saint-Exupéry, The Little Prince


Gamer. Cosplayer. World-Changer.


I was a certified paralegal in a previous life, but it led me to question my own value to not only my company but more importantly, my value to the world- I was too awesome to be stuffed into a cubicle! I have always led my life with this belief that everyone could make a difference in the smallest ways but when I lost sight of that belief, I knew it was time to reevaluate where I was and what I was doing. So I reconnected with my root interest in helping people and being a creator and uniting it with my interest in the human condition...which led me to UX and a newfound passion for making the world a better place.

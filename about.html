What is a Nicole?

“A rock pile ceases to be a rock pile the moment a single man contemplates it, bearing within him the image of a cathedral.” ― Antoine de Saint-Exupéry, The Little Prince


Well...you see...

My job as a paralegal led me to question my own value to not only my company but more importantly, my value to the world. I have always led my life with this belief that everyone could make a difference in seemingly mundane and simple acts but when I lost sight of that belief, I knew it was time to reevaluate where I was in life. So I reconnected with my root interest in visual design and united it with my love for the human condition and so my dive into UX was set.
